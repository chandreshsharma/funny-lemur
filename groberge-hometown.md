## Manchester, NH

### Great Places to Eat
- Belmont Hall
  - Great for breakfast
- Ceasario's
  - Amazing french fries
- Pizza Express
  - Steak tip salad is a favorite
- Goldenrod's
  - Best ice cream and fried seafood
- Milk and Honey
  - Vegetarian friendly

### Fun Things to Do
- NH Fishercats
  - Minor league baseball
- Elm Street
  - Many shops and restaurants
- Spare Time
  - Ten-pin bowling lanes
